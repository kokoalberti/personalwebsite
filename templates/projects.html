Maptip

Maptip is a geospatial search and recommendation engine for books and maps. The concept is stupidly simple: pan your map to an area of interest and it will give you a list of books and maps relevant for that area.

Check out the Maptip website
Flask-TrelloBoard Extension

The Flask-TrelloBoard extension allows you to source content for a Flask application from a Trello board. You'll be surprised how well a Trello board can function as a basic content management system.
